Computing Bio

Here is my history with computing

I have been interested in computers and computing since I was 12. My aunt bought me my very first computer - an IBM PC with a 286 processor. It was from this early PC that my fascination with computers arose.

I like the hardware aspect of computers more than software and programming. Actually tinkering with components and building a computer gives me great satisfaction.
